# Parmesan Chicken

## Ingredients
- chicken breast: 2 pieces
- breadcrumbs: 100 g
- parmesan cheese: 50 g
- eggs: 2
- salt: 1 tsp
- olive oil: 3 tbsp

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
2. In a shallow bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt.
3. In another shallow bowl, mix the breadcrumbs and grated parmesan cheese.
4. Dip each chicken breast first into the whisked eggs, then press into the breadcrumbs and parmesan mixture until fully and evenly coated.
5. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the coated chicken breasts for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and has reached an internal temperature of 74°C (165°F).
7. Serve warm, optionally topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.
